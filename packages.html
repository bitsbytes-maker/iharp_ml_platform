CMAD

v0.1.0.post6  ·  Python ≥ 3.8  ·  License: iHARP

A lightweight, unsupervised framework for detecting clusters of extreme negative spatio-temporal anomalies in sequential 2D data. Designed for applications such as sea ice melt detection, environmental degradation, and geospatial change analysis. Requires no training labels and adapts automatically to arbitrary spatial resolutions.
